Why you're not showing up — what we found
🔍
No content for Google to rank
Google ranks pages that answer what people are searching for. There are no blog posts, guides, or FAQ pages on your site. Competitors like GeeM Home and We Will Fix It publish content regularly — that's why they appear and Villacare Plus doesn't.
📄
Service pages aren't optimised for any keyword
Your homepage title is simply "Home – Villacare Plus." No Dubai, no villas, no maintenance. Each service page needs to be written and structured around the exact words customers type — so Google knows to show it.
🔗
Three service pages are broken
Pergola, LVT Flooring, and Electrical pages link to "#" — they go nowhere. Google cannot rank a page that doesn't properly exist, no matter how strong your actual service is.
🌐
No backlinks — Google doesn't fully trust your site yet
Backlinks are other websites linking to yours — Google's main signal of credibility. Without them, even a well-optimised page struggles to rank. Your competitors have links from UAE property directories, home blogs, and media. You have none yet.
⭐
62 five-star reviews that Google can't display
Without review schema markup, your star rating doesn't appear in search results. Competitors with fewer reviews are showing theirs — and getting more clicks because of it. A quick technical fix changes this.
🤖
Invisible to AI — ChatGPT and Google AI don't know you exist
When someone asks ChatGPT "best villa maintenance company in Dubai," it recommends brands it has learned about from articles and citations online. Villacare Plus has none of that yet — so AI doesn't recommend you. This is the fastest-growing way people find services in 2026.
